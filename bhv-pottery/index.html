ABOUT BHV POTTERY

BHV Pottery is a Vietnamese enterprise specialized in planters and decoration items of various materials and designs. One of our target is to become your long-term reliable supplier whom you can trust. To do this, BHV Pottery always pays careful attention to every aspects from the production, quality control to packing so that we can assure high quality and customers' satisfaction.

BHV Pottery also believes that continuous innovation can lead us to success and help to meet various demands of the customers. With that maxim, BHV is proud that we have successfully developed the famous ranges of Lite Oceania, Hi-grade Lacquer Planters and UV-Terracotta.

We are always willing to contribute to the development of your new projects and hope that you can find all you need at BHV Pottery.
